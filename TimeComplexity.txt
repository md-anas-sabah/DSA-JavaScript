Objects-Big-O
1. Insert: O(1)
2. Remove: O(1)
3. Access: O(1)
4. Search: O(n)
5. Object.keys(): O(n)
6. Object.values(): O(n)
7. Object.entries(): O(n)


Arrays-Big-O
1. Insert/Remove at end: O(1)
2. Insert/Remove at beginning: O(n)
3. Access: O(1)
4. Search: O(n)
5. Push/Pop: O(1)
6. Shift/UnShift/Concat/Slice/Splice: O(n)
7. forEach/map/filter/reduce: O(n).
